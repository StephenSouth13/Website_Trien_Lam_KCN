About VSM Expo 2025

Vinh Long Industrial Supply Manufacturing Expo (VSM Expo 2025) is a pioneering event aimed at promoting the development of the local supply chain, creating an effective trading platform for businesses inside and outside the province.

With over 200 booths and an expected attendance of 5,000+ visitors, VSM Expo 2025 promises to bring outstanding opportunities for cooperation and business development.
