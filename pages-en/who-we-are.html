Welcome to VSM Expo 2025

VSM Expo 2025 was born from the aspiration to build a sustainable connection platform for the manufacturing and supply business community in the Mekong Delta, with Vinh Long as its hub.

We recognized the immense but not fully tapped potential of the local supply chain. Thus, VSM Expo was created to become a prestigious annual event where businesses can meet, showcase technology, find partners, and collectively boost the region's industrial economy.

The first-ever large-scale event brings together industry leaders, policymakers, and hundreds of representative businesses, promising to create a powerful stimulus for the development of industrial parks in Vinh Long and neighboring provinces.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become the leading and most influential industrial supply exhibition in the Mekong Delta region by 2030, serving as an indispensable bridge for domestic and international businesses.

Mission

To create a professional and efficient trading environment that helps businesses optimize their supply chains, enhance competitiveness, and contribute to the sustainable development of Vietnam's industry.
